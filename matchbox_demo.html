About Matchbox

Small form factor

Built around a Raspberry Pi 5, Matchbox is a compact, always-on appliance designed to fit anywhere in your home while providing genuine computing power.

Privacy-first

No app stores, no proprietary relay services, no ad-tech business models. Your data stays under your roof, under your control.

Dual NVMe storage

Two separate M.2 NVMe SSDs: one for the OS, one for your data. Your files stay safe from system changes, and the OS can be updated or replaced without touching your storage.

Self-hosted

Run tasks, photos, messaging, and web services from hardware you own. No subscriptions, no surveillance, no permission needed.
